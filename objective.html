THE PROBLEM

Problem 1:

India has more than 30 million stray dogs with more than 20,000 people dying of rabies every year.
Also, there is a huge surge in animal abuse cases involving street dogs.
These dogs are usually not kept in a good environment and are ill-treated even in dog shelters and MCD dog-houses.

Problem 2:

Mental Health cases are rising all over the country and due to the lockdown therapies have also become ineffectual.
With the rising economy of the country, the stress levels are increasing at an alarming rate.
This stress, if not treated, may even cause clinical depression in certain cases.

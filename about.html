It all started with recognising the problem.It'll blow your mind.

Theradog is a two-way beneficiary idea, in which not only the ailing and depressed humans are being helped using Animal-Assisted Therapies ,
but also the street dogs who don’t have any owner, who don’t have a family and are not fed properly,
start earning for themselves at a very early age. Animal-Assisted Therapy looks and works almost
like any other therapy session, except for the animal component.

We started in 2020 and growing.

Tummy tickles and cute hugs, nothing beats puppy therapies! We will have a place where the right environment
for the therapy will be set up and therapies will be provided there in CCTV surveillance and supervision.

Goals of Pet Therapy and plans for future.

Pet therapy builds on the pre-existing human-animal bond.
Interacting with a friendly pet can help many physical and mental issues.
It can help reduce blood pressure and improve overall cardiovascular health.
It can also release endorphins that produce a calming effect.

Profitability

REVENUE Major source of revenue is through the therapies booked and if a customer really loves the experience,
he/she can buy and take the highly trained therapy dog to their home. Through App-based ads of mental health audio books and audio sessions. Adding premium subscription features to the app at a later stage.
